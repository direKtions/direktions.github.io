About us

direKtions

At direKtions, we are striving to create tech-savvy professionals that are equipped with the latest IT technologies and research tools to face the challenges of the future. With this aim, we work with individuals as well as organizations to help them achieve their goals.

Our Story

In 2018, the founders of direKtions came up with the idea of setting up a platform which could provide a liaison between academia and industry. With all founders holding advanced degrees in computer science from world-renowned universities and having a sound track record in academia, research, and industry, they founded direKtions in 2019.
